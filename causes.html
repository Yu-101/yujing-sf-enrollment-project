Main Causes of Ocean Pollution

Ocean pollution comes from human action. Here are the major causes:

  • Plastic Waste: Over 8 million tons of plastic enter the ocean yearly.
  • Oil Spills: Oil damages birds, fish, coral, and blocks sunlight.
  • Chemical Pollution: Pesticides, detergents, and factory waste poison water.
  • Overfishing: Marine life collapses when fish cannot reproduce fast enough.
